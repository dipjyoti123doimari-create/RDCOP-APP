🚀 Quick Start
  1. Go to Data Entry — select month/year, then enter EB meter, DG and Mixer readings for each plant.
  2. Go to Calculate — select the date range and run to compute energy consumption and DG ratios.
  3. Go to View Reports — see plant-wise & location-wise tables, download Excel or email.
💡 Plant names & locations are pulled from RDC-TP plant data. If plants are missing, go to RDC-TP → Data Uploader to sync them first. Total Volume (MT) is fetched from the Oracle TP data table by plant code.
